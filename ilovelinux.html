平時整備與訓練：建立大腦、身體與資產的防火牆

  • 自主防護與居家堡壘
    民防的核心在於「主動」。對於老弱婦孺或行動不便者，在極端災害發生、交通停擺時移動反而會增加風險。因此，「就地避難」並在家中儲備足夠的糧食與慢性病藥物，就是最安全的選擇。「照顧好自己，不增添避難所與社會的負擔，就是對民防最大的貢獻。」
  • 建立「金融韌性」
    錢也是物資的一種。劉教授特別提醒，面對重大無預警危機，我們必須準備「金融避難包」——在家中或身上備妥現金，以應對斷電導致 ATM 停擺或信用卡失效的窘境。
  • 資訊過濾
    在假訊息橫行的時代，建立「信任機構名單」是最重要的心防演練，透過理性的訊息過濾機制，我們才能在動盪中保持冷靜。
  • Self-Protection and the Home Fortress
    The core of civil defense is "proactivity." For the elderly, disabled, or those with mobility issues, moving during extreme disasters or transportation disruptions can actually increase risks. Therefore, "sheltering in place" and stocking up on sufficient food and chronic medications at home is the safest choice. "Taking care of yourself and not adding to the burden of shelters and society is the greatest contribution to civil defense."
  • Building "Financial Resilience"
    Money is also a type of resource. Professor Liu particularly reminds us that in the face of major unforeseen crises, we must prepare a "financial evacuation kit"—keeping cash at home or on hand to cope with situations where power outages lead to ATM shutdowns or credit card failures.
  • Information Filtering
    In an era rampant with misinformation, establishing a "trusted institution list" is the most important mental defense exercise. Through a rational information filtering mechanism, we can maintain calm amidst turmoil.

  • 積極撤離

    「逃」具有積極的避險意義。有組織地向安全地帶撤離是減少傷亡的首要任務，以保留社區復原的火種。


  • 社區巡守

    災害期間，基層巡守隊協助治安與物資調度，在政府資源抵達前，確保鄰里不因恐慌而陷入混亂。


  • 合法授權

    民防運作必須建立在合法性之上。所有救災行為皆須符合內政部規範，避免產生法律爭議，建立大眾信任。

  • Active Evacuation

    Evacuation is a proactive form of risk mitigation. Organized movement to safe zones is the primary task to reduce casualties and preserve the hope for community recovery.


  • Community Patrols

    During disasters, grassroots patrol teams assist in maintaining public order and coordinating resources, ensuring neighborhoods do not fall into chaos before government resources arrive.


  • Legal Authorization

    Civil defense operations must be built upon legality. All disaster relief actions must comply with Ministry of the Interior regulations to avoid legal disputes and build public trust.
