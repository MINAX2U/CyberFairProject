To gain an in-depth understanding of the operational aspects of civilian defense, our team conducted an exclusive interview with "High Mute (S.T.A)", a group composed of retired personnel.

They revealed to us the original intentions behind their training in action and crisis awareness.

組織初衷：建立危機感知，為社會買一份「保險」

高默（S.T.A）是由台灣退役特戰成員組成的民間訓練團體。在我們的訪談中，教官點出一個關鍵：「台灣人幾乎都經歷過颱風、地震等自然災害，但極少人具備應對複合型極端危機的『臨場感知』。」面對網路上「推廣民防是在製造恐慌」的質疑，創辦人語重心長地向我們比喻：「這就像幫小孩買保險，不是在挑戰死神；做健康檢查，也不是在挑釁病菌。」

追求平安不能只靠口號，提升自身的應變實力，才是面對未知風險的最佳解答。三年來，高默成功帶領跨性別、跨背景的平民克服體能限制，建立起「保護自己、伸出援手」的基礎能力。

High Mute (S.T.A) is a civilian training group composed of retired special operations members in Taiwan. During our interview, the instructor pointed out a key issue: "Almost all Taiwanese have experienced natural disasters such as typhoons and earthquakes, but very few possess the 'on-site awareness' to respond to complex extreme crises." In response to online criticism that "promoting civil defense is creating panic," the founder earnestly compared it to "buying insurance for children, which is not challenging death; doing health check-ups, which is not provoking germs."

Pursuing safety cannot rely solely on slogans; enhancing one's own response capabilities is the best answer to facing unknown risks. Over the past three years, High Mute has successfully led civilians across gender and backgrounds to overcome physical limitations and establish the basic ability to "protect themselves and lend a helping hand."

運作模式：從單點突破到系統化教育

高默的訓練與其他單位相比，更強調「精緻化」。教官向我們分享，這三年來，課程從最初每個月一兩次，發展到現在幾乎每週都有課。他們設計了嚴謹的 Level 分級訓練，從單項技術到整體考核，讓學生在循序漸進中獲得成就感。

此外，學員結訓後會形成緊密的支援小組，大家私下甚至會交流睡袋、背包等二手裝備。這種強大的社群凝聚力，讓有志於防災的人不再感到孤單，也破除了外界對於民防團體是「激進好戰分子」的誤解。

High Mute's training, compared to other organizations, places a stronger emphasis on "refinement." Instructors shared with us that over the past three years, the curriculum has evolved from initially occurring once or twice a month to nearly weekly sessions. They have designed rigorous Level-based training programs, from individual skills to comprehensive assessments, allowing students to gain a sense of accomplishment through progressive learning.

Additionally, graduates form tight-knit support groups, and members even exchange used equipment like sleeping bags and backpacks. This strong sense of community ensures that those interested in disaster preparedness no longer feel isolated and helps dispel misconceptions about civil defense groups being "radical activists."

社群連結：與地方共榮的智慧

作為扎根地方的社群團體，高默極度重視行動的「合法性」與「在地連結」。

在訪談中我們得知一個生動的幕後故事：為了不讓拿著訓練器材的學員嚇到一般民眾，每次演習前，高默都會與當地村里長及警察單位充分溝通。「剛好我外公以前是那邊的村長，有地緣關係。」創辦人笑著說。更棒的是，高默的訓練活動成功帶動了當地的露營區及住宿業發展。

這種「民防帶動地方產業」的共生模式，不僅讓在地居民從陌生轉為支持，更吸引了許多軍警消人員利用休假以平民身分來進修精進。

As a community group rooted in the local area, High Mute places great emphasis on the "legitimacy" and "local connections" of its actions.

In our interviews, we learned a vivid behind-the-scenes story: to avoid startling ordinary citizens with training equipment, High Mute always communicates thoroughly with local village heads and police units before each exercise. "It just so happens that my grandfather was the village chief there, so I have a local connection." The founder said with a smile. Even better, High Mute's training activities have successfully promoted the development of local camping areas and the hospitality industry.

This "civil defense driving local industry" symbiotic model not only helps local residents transition from unfamiliarity to support but also attracts many military, police, and emergency responders to use their vacations to pursue further training.

未來展望：走進社區，內化「不驚慌」的社會文化

高默的終極願景是將防災觀念深植人心。教官在受訪時生動地比喻：「民防的終極目標就是建立『不要驚慌』的觀念。就像飛機出狀況，你不能尖叫，要聽從空服員指揮；也不能像疫情期間，發燒 39 度就全衝急診，造成醫療資源浪費。」

透過持續的專業推廣，高默期許讓每位公民在天災或緊急狀況發生時，都能具備判斷輕重緩急的能力。學會配合政府專業部門，在自救的同時也成為社會穩定的力量。

這份展望不只是技術傳承，更是對社會責任的承諾，致力於讓每一位民眾都成為強化社會安全防護網中，最堅實且理性的後盾。

High Mute's ultimate vision is to deeply embed the concept of disaster preparedness in people's hearts. During the interview, the instructor vividly compared it to "the ultimate goal of civil defense is to establish the concept of 'not panicking.' Just like when a plane has an issue, you can't scream; you have to follow the flight attendants' instructions. You also can't rush to the emergency room just because you have a fever of 39 degrees during a pandemic, which causes a waste of medical resources."

Through continuous professional promotion, High Mute hopes to enable every citizen to have the ability to judge the urgency of situations during natural disasters or emergencies. They aim for people to learn how to cooperate with government professional departments, becoming a force for social stability while also being able to self-rescue.

This vision is not just about technical inheritance but also a commitment to social responsibility, dedicated to making every citizen the most solid and rational support in strengthening the social safety net.
